About Me

I am an aspiring coder with an extensive background in public education. I am coming off a 20-year career teaching Algebra 1 in a public charter school in Houston, TX. During my time in education, I received numerous accolades and held a variety of leadership roles. However, the hours and stress was beginning to take a toll on my mental and physical health. I made the difficult decision to leave the profession and return to Ohio to "start over" on a new journey. I have always enjoyed computers and have some experience in video production. I am excited to see where this new journey takes me!
